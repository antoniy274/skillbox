According to statistics, every eleventh adult is an onychophagist. The reasons for this phenomenon are not fully understood, but it is obvious that they are of a psychological nature. As early as 1893, it was established that one of the reasons is nervousness. There are original hypotheses: an attempt to punish oneself for obscene and stupid thoughts, a symbolic reprisal against one's own aggressiveness and a hidden desire for self-destruction.
